Before you apply

Your opportunity as a Fresh and Shine cleaner.

We are building a trusted database of cleaners we can recommend and supply when hospitality clients require cleaning staff.

  • Work in cleaning services that Fresh and Shine already provides.
  • Be considered for hotel, Airbnb, office, household and laundry opportunities.
  • Share your real availability, service areas, skills and references.
  • Receive follow-up by phone, email or SMS if your application is shortlisted.

Requirements

  • You must have a working smartphone and WhatsApp.
  • You must be 18 years or older.
  • You must have a South African ID, valid work permit, refugee ID or residency document.
  • You must be willing to complete reference and background checks.
  • You must be reliable, presentable and able to travel to selected work areas.
