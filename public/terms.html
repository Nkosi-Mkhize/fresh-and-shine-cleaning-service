Terms and Conditions

These starter terms describe how bookings, quotes and service requests are handled by Fresh and Shine Cleaning Service.

Bookings

Online bookings are requests until Fresh and Shine confirms availability, final price and payment details.

Client responsibilities

Clients should provide accurate access instructions, safe working conditions, correct property details and any required linen or cleaning-specific supplies where agreed.

Payments

Yoco, PayFast and EFT payment methods can be supported. Final payment instructions are confirmed after booking review unless a live payment gateway is connected.

Quotes

Hotel, guesthouse, Airbnb portfolio and larger commercial quotes are prepared based on room count, frequency, scope and location.
